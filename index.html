The Smarter Way To Learn Anything

Bani Bandana Tutorials is a private coaching centre where students of all classes are taught. The main purpose of Bani Bandana Tutorials is to prepare students to enhance their intellectual, physical, social, emotional and spiritual growth so that they may realize their power as good citizens and always help and work for the people.

Subjects Taught In Our Coaching Centre

All subjects starting from Class KG - Class 10 are taught. The main subjects taught here are Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English, Bengali, Hindi, Computer(Not Practical), History, Geography. Our teacher teaches each and every subject in simple language and with the help of General Knowledge and Stories which help the students understand better. Daily, homework is given for the students which increases the efficiency and helps the students to practice more to understand and learn better.

Science

The subjects which come under the "Science" Group are "Physics", "Chemistry", "Biology" and "Mathematics".

Computer

In Bani Bandana Tutorials, Computer is also taught. Only theory is taught. If you want to do practial, bring your device with you.

Literature

The subjects which come under the "Literature" Group are "English", "Bengali" and "Hindi".
